------ choix des paramètres ------
- perplexity : valeur (5,50,5) ; larger datasets usually require a larger perplexity, it can be defined as the balance between preserving the global and the local structure of the data.
- early_exaggeration : it increases the attractive forces between points and allowd points to move around more freely, finding their nearest beightbors more easily.
- max_iter (default=1000), à changer si l'iteration est trop longue
